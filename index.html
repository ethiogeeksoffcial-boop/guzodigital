FAQ

Common Questions

How long until I see results?
Most clients start seeing measurable improvements within the first 30 days. Significant results typically show at the 60–90 day mark as campaigns mature.
Do I need to provide content or do you handle everything?
We handle everything — writing, design, video editing, and posting. You simply review and approve content before it goes live.
Do you work with businesses outside of Harar?
Absolutely. While we're based in Harar, we serve clients across Ethiopia and internationally. All collaboration is done remotely via WhatsApp, Telegram, and video calls.
What industries do you work with?
We work with restaurants, hotels, fashion, real estate, e-commerce, healthcare, education, NGOs, and more. If you have a business and a target audience, we can help.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes. We work on a monthly basis with no long-term contracts. We believe in earning your business every month through results — not lock-ins.
Do you offer one-time projects?
Yes! We offer one-time services including logo design, website builds, brand identity packages, and individual ad campaign setups. Contact us for a custom quote.
